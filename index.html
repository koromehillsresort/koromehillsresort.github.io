    About Us

    Discover the perfect blend of nature and luxury at Korome Hills Resort, nestled in the stunning landscapes of Wayanad. Experience unparalleled comfort and breathtaking views in a serene, fully-equipped getaway.

    Looking for a vacation in one of the most scenic beauties you can find?

    Then Korome Hills is definitely the right place for you. Placed amongst the greenery and otherworldy view of Wayanad, Korome Hills Resort offers the best of nature in the comfort of a fully facilitated resort. Complete with an outdoor pool that everyone can enjoy in and a restaurant that offers a variety of dishes based on your preference. Korome Hills also provides private parking for those who come in their own vehicle, but for those who want to rent one to tour the surrounding area, car rental is available. With leisure amenities, such as a TV in each room and a garden right outside, there is relaxation guaranteed both indoors and outdoors. So, what are you waiting for? Book your stay and enjoy all that Korome Hills has to offer!
